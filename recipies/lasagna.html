Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is an Italian dish made with a tomato-based sauce and meat. You will also make a beschamel or a cheese sauce. These sauces are distributed in layers separated by pasta sheets. The finishing touches are a bunch of grated cheese on the topmost layer and som fresh basil leaves after the baking.

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below.
